# Workflow — Platform: Instagram

## ISOLATION RULES — READ FIRST

Instagram is a **completely separate audience** from all B2B channels.

- Audience: friends, family, personal circle
- NO Setu mention, NO agency branding, NO AI automation content
- NO cross-posts from LinkedIn, X, or YouTube
- Tone: personal, funny, creative — not professional

This is intentionally a separate feed. Do not mix. Do not hint at the agency. This is personal Instagram, not a brand account.

## Cadence
- 2-3 posts/week
- Reels preferred over static posts (IG algorithm favors video)
- Stories: ad hoc, not tracked in posts.json unless notable

## Format rules

### Reels (preferred)
- Under 60 seconds
- Hook in first 2 seconds (visual or spoken)
- One concept — don't cram multiple bits
- Captions: auto-generated, review for accuracy before publishing

### Static posts / carousels
- Carousel: funny visual series, max 7 slides, punchline on last slide
- Static: for strong one-image moments. Caption is the joke.

### Caption style (this feed)
- Short and punchy. 1-3 sentences.
- The bit should land in the caption, not in a long paragraph.
- 3-5 relevant hashtags (IG hashtags actually work here, unlike X)
- Emojis: fine here. Not excessive.

## Content ideas (examples, not rules)
- Tech industry absurdity (without Setu/AI context)
- Relatable founder/developer observations
- Everyday roasting (traffic, meetings, Indian startup culture)
- Random funny observations

## Publishing
Phase 1-2: via Blotato (connected to IG personal account).
Phase 3+: via `tools/instagram_post.py` (Meta Graph API — requires Meta developer account).

## Metrics
Not tracked in the main auditor loop. Check manually monthly. This feed is not optimized for growth — it exists for authentic personal presence.
