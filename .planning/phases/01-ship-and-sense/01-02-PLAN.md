Banned words: revolutionary, game-changing, disrupt, synergy, cutting-edge, "empowering businesses to unlock potential"

Format rules (LinkedIn):
- Hook = first line only (no complete sentence, pattern interrupt)
- 150-300 words optimal
- Line breaks every 1-3 lines
- 1-2 hashtags max
- No links in body (first comment only)
- End with open question or CTA
