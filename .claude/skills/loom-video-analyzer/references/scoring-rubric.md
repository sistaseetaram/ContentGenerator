# Loom Video Scoring Rubric v0.1

Each dimension is scored independently. Total = 10 points.

---

## Hook (0–2 points)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 2 | Hook lands in first 10s. Outcome-first. No filler ("so today...", "hi everyone"). Matches or improves on prep hook. Single clear sentence. |
| 1.5 | Hook lands but slightly slow (10–15s before the point). Minor filler. |
| 1 | Hook present but buried. Starts with context before the punch. |
| 0.5 | Weak open. Generic intro. No outcome signal in first 15s. |
| 0 | No identifiable hook. Rambling start. |

**Calibration note:** Outcome-first means the $0.06 or "94% cost reduction" comes before the explanation of how, not after.

---

## Beat coverage (0–3 points)

Score = (beats covered fully / total beats) × 3, rounded to nearest 0.5.

| Coverage | Score |
|----------|-------|
| 100% | 3.0 |
| 80% | 2.5 |
| 60% | 2.0 |
| 40% | 1.5 |
| 20% | 0.5 |
| 0% | 0 |

"Covered fully" = the beat aim was achieved AND at least 2 of the 3–5 talking points appeared (not necessarily verbatim — paraphrase counts).

**Calibration note:** A beat that was mentioned but not demonstrated (said "I separated static data" but didn't show it) = 50% credit only.

---

## Numbers accuracy (0–1 point)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 1 | All key numbers from prep cited correctly. No wrong figures. |
| 0.5 | Most numbers correct. 1 number missing or slightly off (rounding). |
| 0 | Key number wrong (e.g. said "$0.10" instead of "$0.06") OR numbers not mentioned at all when they were in prep. |

Numbers to check: pulled from prep packet. Typically cost figures, %, time estimates.

---

## Brand voice (0–2 points)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 2 | Zero banned words. Short sentences (≤12 words per thought, most of the time). Quiet confident tone throughout. No apologising for past versions. No hype energy. |
| 1.5 | One minor voice slip — single filler phrase or slightly long sentence. No banned words. |
| 1 | 1–2 voice issues — mild hedging ("kind of", "sort of"), 1 borderline banned word, or 1 apology for an early version. |
| 0.5 | Multiple voice violations — consistent hedging, long rambling sentences, or clear hype moment. |
| 0 | Banned word used, or significant hype/apology energy that misrepresents the brand. |

**Banned words:** revolutionary, game-changing, disrupt, synergy, cutting-edge, "empowering businesses to unlock potential"

**Calibration note:** Saying "I'm excited to show you" once = 0.5 deduction. Saying it twice = 1 point deduction.

---

## Brand values (0–1 point)

Score = (values passed / 5) rounded to nearest 0.5.

Check each of the five values against the transcript:

| Value | Pass condition |
|-------|----------------|
| Work, not tech | Led with outcomes (time, money, workflow) — not model names or API specs |
| Quiet over loud | Calm, confident tone. No hype. No manifesto language. |
| Respect owners | Treated the viewer as smart. No lecturing. No condescension. |
| Ship, don't slide | Showed something running or deployed. Not just planned. |
| Map before build | Showed the reasoning/diagnosis step, not just the answer. |

**Hard cap:** If any value is clearly violated (not just slightly missed), this dimension = 0.

---

## Close + CTA (0–1 point)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 1 | Close line landed clearly. CTA was specific (question, follow, DM). Ended clean — no trailing ramble. |
| 0.5 | Close present but muddied. CTA vague or missing. |
| 0 | No identifiable close. Video just stops or trails off. |
